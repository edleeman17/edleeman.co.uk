Sift is a Raspberry Pi bridge between your iPhone and a dumbphone. It listens for notifications over Bluetooth, runs them through a configurable AI filtering pipeline, and forwards only the ones that actually matter via SMS.

The goal: get the focus benefits of a dumbphone without missing genuinely important messages. You define the rules — Sift does the triage.

**Tech:** Python, Bluetooth LE, Raspberry Pi, AI classification, SMS gateway
