A self-hosted RSS reader built specifically for e-ink screens. The UI prioritises readability over aesthetics — high contrast, generous touch targets, no transitions or animations that look terrible on e-paper.

Runs in Docker, stores feeds in SQLite, supports OPML import/export, and auto-refreshes on a schedule.

**Tech:** Python, Flask, SQLite, feedparser, APScheduler, Docker
